Alliance of Therapy Dogs

Alliance of Therapy Dogs is an international registry of certified therapy dog teams. They provide testing, certification, registration, support, and insurance for members who volunteer with their dogs in animal-assisted activities.

Visit Website »

Bright and Beautiful Therapy Dogs

The Bright and Beautiful Therapy Dogs, Inc. is a nonprofit organization which evaluates, tests, trains and qualifies owners and their well behaved dogs as therapy dog teams. These teams give unconditional love, boost self-esteem, and relieve loneliness and boredom. They work in nursing homes, hospitals, psychiatric wards, shelters, schools and many other facilities.

Visit Website »

Love on a Leash

Love on a Leash is committed to bringing comfort, happiness, and healing to more people nationwide by increasing public awareness of pet-provided therapy. Their volunteer organization provides an accessible certification process that allows qualified therapy teams to provide effective pet-provided therapy services in their community.

Therapy Dogs: And their impact on anxiety and depression.

If you struggle with anxiety or depression, you understand how debilitating these disorders can be. However, therapy dogs are able to significantly help through providing unconditional love, companionship, and a sense of purpose for their owners.

Therapy Dogs: We are not the same as Service Dogs!

A service dog helps individuals perform tasks they cannot do for themselves because of a disability. The Americans with Disabilities Act governs the use of service dogs in public places. Therapy dogs are there to provide comfort and support to their companion.

All Dogs Go to Heaven

Any dog can become a therapy dog, no matter what breed. The ingredients that make a great therapy dog are personality and training!
